40 OVER FORTY list is live!

The inaugural 40 OVER FORTY list is now live!

Massive congratulations to everyone who made the first-ever 40 Over Forty list. Thank you to everyone who voted and who is being part of the change against ageism. We’ve been overwhelmed by the support and positive feedback we’ve received.

This platform was created because the advertising, marketing and media industries are obsessed with youth, and we want to change that obsession to make it a more level playing field, by acknowledging experienced marketers that we can all learn from.

Our purpose is to celebrate your number, talent and valuable experience and this year’s list really showcases the great, diverse talent out there.

We are in the creative industry, where diversity is essential to our success - and we are passionate about celebrating everyone’s talents and those who have inspired us on our own career journeys.

There is more to come from 40 Over Forty very soon, so watch this space!
